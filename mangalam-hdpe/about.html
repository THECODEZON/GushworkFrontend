Our Story

From a Single Unit to
a National Leader

Mangalam HDPE Pipes was established with a singular vision — to bring world-class piping infrastructure to India's growing economy. Starting from a single manufacturing unit in Pune, Maharashtra, we have expanded into a state-of-the-art 15-acre production facility with a capacity exceeding 18,000 metric tonnes per year.

Every pipe leaves our factory only after passing three stages of quality testing — conforming to IS 5984, ISO 4427, and ASTM D3035 standards. Our commitment to zero-defect manufacturing is backed by 100% batch traceability from raw material to dispatch.

Our Values

What We Stand For

Every decision — from sourcing to delivery — is shaped by four principles that have defined us since day one.

01

Quality Without Compromise

Zero-defect manufacturing, 100% batch traceability from raw material to dispatch, and triple-stage QC before every shipment leaves the factory.

02

Sustainability First

100% recyclable HDPE, solar-powered production halls, closed-loop water recycling — our environmental commitment starts at the machine, not the marketing brochure.

Our Journey

Three Decades of Growth

Every milestone reflects our relentless pursuit of quality, innovation, and scale.

1994
Founded

The Beginning

First manufacturing unit established in Pune, Maharashtra — focused on water-supply piping for municipal and residential projects.

1999
Government

First State Contract

Won Maharashtra's rural water distribution scheme — our first large-scale state government project, supplying 200+ km of HDPE pipeline.

2002
Certification

BIS Certified

Bureau of Indian Standards certification for IS 4984 — a landmark milestone cementing our commitment to manufacturing quality.

2008
Expansion

15-Acre Facility

Expanded to our current facility — increasing annual output to 10,000+ MT and introducing PE 100 grade pipes for gas distribution.

2015
Certification

ISO 9001:2015 & Exports

Secured ISO certification and CE Mark — opening export markets in South-East Asia, Africa, and the Middle East.

2023
Today

18,000 MT Capacity

Record production, 1,200+ active clients, 28-state distribution network, and our largest-ever export shipment — the journey continues.
